General Guidelines


1. Participation is open to all UG / PG students.
2. Registration is from 08:30 A.M to 10:00 A.M
3. All students are required to produce their College ID card or consent letter from the College.
4. No restriction for the number of teams from a College.
5. Team should comprise of participants from the same College/Institution.
6. Replacement of any participant in a team is not allowed after registration at venue.
7. Decision of the judges and core committee is final.
8. There will be no refund of participation fee under any circumstances.
9. Accommodation is provided only for the qualifiers for second day.
10. Registration fee is ₹ 300 for both Participants and Non - Participants and includes food and refreshments.
11. Transportation will be provided to the participants from Kannur railway station and new bus stand.
12. Usage of mobile phone is strictly prohibited during the time of events unless to be used in an event. Smart phones are mandatory for some events.
13. The participants are requested to maintain the decorum of the Institution. If any misbehaviour and malpractices are noted, it will lead to disqualification of the whole team.
14. Participants are required to take care of their belongings. The College authorities will not be responsible for any loss/damage to the belongings.
15. All participants are suggested to register online on or before 20 th Nov 2016 for easy management from our end. URL:- www.chintech.ac.in
